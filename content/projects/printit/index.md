<a href="https://printit.ink"><strong>Printit</strong> </a> is a cloud printing service that allows students and professionals to print documents on any print shop without needing go through instant messaging softwares and personal identification numbers, while providing a featureful user experience. It is designed to be convenient, efficient and private making it the go to app for printing needs in educational and business settings.
